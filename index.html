About Me

Welcome! I am a PhD student in Finance at the Business School, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). My research focuses on Macro-Finance, Financial Intermediation, and Corporate Finance.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I was a Pre-Doctoral Fellow at the Centre for Advanced Financial Research and Learning (CAFRAL), Reserve Bank of India (June 2024 – November 2025). I hold an MA in Economics from the Delhi School of Economics, University of Delhi (2022–2024), and a B.Tech in Civil Engineering from the Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore (2018–2022).
